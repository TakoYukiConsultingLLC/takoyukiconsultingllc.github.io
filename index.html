About TakoYuki Consulting LLC

Led by Industry Expert Erwin Lina

TakoYuki Consulting LLC was established on December 10, 2025, with a mission to provide high-quality, compliant, and results-driven credit repair outsourcing solutions.

With over 10 years of hands-on experience in credit repair, our founder has successfully handled thousands of disputes including collections, charge-offs, late payments, repossessions, and banking records.

Every dispute is personally managed and overseen—from analysis to execution—ensuring that every case follows factual-based strategies and Metro 2 compliance standards.

Our goal is simple: deliver real, measurable results while helping credit repair businesses scale without worrying about backend operations.

If you're looking for a reliable partner who understands the system and delivers consistent results— you are in the right place.
